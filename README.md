# /data summary:

**TWS_data.csv**
Field measurements conducted in the Taiwan Strait, including phytoplankton primary production, bacterial metabolism, and associated environmental parameters, were obtained from sampling stations across seven cruises carried out from 2006 to 2022.
- **No**: Sampling station number
- **Longitude**: Geographic longitude (°E)
- **Latitude**: Geographic latitude (°N)
- **Date**: Date of sampling
- **Zeu**: Depth of the euphotic zone (m)
- **Watermass**: Water mass category identified by cluster analysis
- **Distco**: Distance from the coastline (km)
- **SST**: Sea surface temperature (°C)
- **SSS**: Sea surface salinity
- **SSChla**: Sea surface chlorophyll-a concentration (mg m⁻³)
- **T**: Depth-averaged temperature within the euphotic zone (°C)
- **S**: Depth-averaged salinity within the euphotic zone
- **DIN**: Depth-averaged dissolved inorganic nitrogen within the euphotic zone (µmol L⁻¹)
- **DIP**: Depth-averaged dissolved inorganic phosphorus within the euphotic zone (µmol L⁻¹)
- **DSi**: Depth-averaged dissolved inorganic silicate within the euphotic zone (µmol L⁻¹)
- **Chla**: Depth-averaged chlorophyll a concentration within the euphotic zone (mg m⁻³)
- **Chla_Micro**: Chlorophyll a attributed to microphytoplankton (>20 µm) (mg m⁻³)
- **Chla_Nano**: Chlorophyll a attributed to nanophytoplankton (2–20 µm) (mg m⁻³)
- **Chla_Pico**: Chlorophyll a attributed to picophytoplankton (0.2–2 µm) (mg m⁻³)
- **PPP**: Depth-averaged particulate primary production in the euphotic zone (mg C m⁻³ d⁻¹)
- **DPP**: Depth-averaged dissolved primary production in the euphotic zone (mg C m⁻³ d⁻¹)
- **TPP**: Depth-averaged total primary production, calculated as the sum of PPP and DPP (mg C m⁻³ d⁻¹)
- **PER**: Fraction of total primary production released as dissolved organic carbon (DPP/TPP)
- **BP**: Depth-averaged bacterial production in the euphotic zone (mg C m⁻³ d⁻¹)
- **BR**: Depth-averaged bacterial respiration in the euphotic zone (mg C m⁻³ d⁻¹)
- **BCD**: Depth-averaged bacterial carbon demand, calculated as BP + BR (mg C m⁻³ d⁻¹)
- **BGE**: Bacterial growth efficiency, calculated as BP/BCD
